OUR TEAM

Welcome to Team Roberto, a group of designers from Politecnico di Milano specializing in interaction design. Formed within the context of the Interaction Design course, our team firmly believes in the educational and communicative power of design and is committed to leveraging it to its fullest extent. With a blend of creativity, technical expertise, and sensitivity, we dedicate ourselves to using design as a tool to promote positive change in society. Through innovative approaches and participatory design methodologies, we aim to create solutions that not only inform and instruct but also inspire and foster open and inclusive dialogue on relevant issues.
