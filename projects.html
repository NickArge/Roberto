Body Shaping

Get ready for an awesome interactive experience made just for you. Step into the photo booth, where you can mix and match different body parts from different statues from Mudec Museum. After you've created your work of art, you'll get a card with your own custom statue on it—make sure to check out the back for some cool facts! BodyShaping is all about breaking the rules on what bodies should look like and getting everyone to chat about how different and awesome we all are. All bodies are beautiful, everyone should know it. So, let's start celebrating everyone's uniqueness and spread the love!
